i started video editing in late 2018! these are the posts that are shown in the main feed. i started in inshot and iMovie, and moved to videostar in early 2019. by the time covid hit, i decided to sit down and learn after effects, and i've stayed there ever since! after effects is truly my favourite program i've used, although it's a bit difficult to learn.

my main account started as callmewtwig (when i made it, i didn't intend to be a video editing account!) and i later changed to twigsfall that december. i ended up changing my name to gastrozard in august of 2021, and that has stuck since! i use this account for anything animated that i edit.

in 2020, i also opened another account named antlvngs! i use this account for live action media that i edit, which is usually marvel (ant-man and the wasp).

antlvngs this is one i tried really hard to make fit with the scenes and the lyrics. i love explaining it to people because i feel like especially with this one, since it's a bit faster, it sort of goes unnoticed. anyways, so since the whole song is "deja vu" which is feeling like you've lived through something before and i really tried to emulate that with this one. while the overall song doesn't really fit with scott + hope's relationship, the "i know you get deja vu" stood out to me which is why i used the scene from amatw as the intro, sort of giving in to that "lived through" experience as hope is recalling the past and therefore their relationship. and then for the four quick beats that are close together, i tried to choose scenes from amatw that half related back to scenes from ant-man with the latter sort of sandwiched between the former. so it would go amatw scene > ant-man scene > ant-man scene > amatw scene. overall i think it came out pretty good for the vision i had! looking back almost 2 years later i wish i had used less blurs lol, but i'm still quite proud of it! and i love love love scotthope <3
