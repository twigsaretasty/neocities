this is just a collection of every song from Legally Blonde (the musical) and how the lyrics changed over time!

At first, I just did this for the Legally Blonde ballad, but I decided to just go for it and look at each of the songs

most info can be found on each page's second box on the lefthand side, but as a rundown:

  • text with no highlights is either part of the first iteration, or wasn't changed from one version to another
  • text with this hightlight is a brand new lyric
  • text with this hightlight is a lyric that had been cut, but was brought back
  • text with this hightlight is a lyric that had been in a different part of the song, but was moved around

some disclaimers because i'm extra

  • not every show was recorded, so the dates/counts may be slightly inaccurate
  • it's also possible that "changes" i take note of weren't real changes, but times where the actors happened to mess up a lyric or two during the show
  • ignore any spelling mistakes. lol
