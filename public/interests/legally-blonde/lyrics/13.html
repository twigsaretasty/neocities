song info

Sung by: Elle, Emmett, Salespeople

Different Versions: 7

Times Each Was Used:

Ver 1: 1
Ver 2: 1
Ver 3: 5 recorded, up to 10
Ver 4: 5 recorded, up to 10
Ver 5: 1 recorded, up to 3
Ver 6: 1 recorded, up to 2
Ver 7: a lot

my thoughts

This is my second favourite song in the musical! I love the vibe of it and it's probably the one I've listened to the most. I love the development it provides for Elle and Emmett's relationship in the way that Chip on My Shoulder did. Instead of Emmett helping Elle, though, it's about Elle helping Emmett. It just highlights how much they can learn from one another since they're pretty different in terms of what they're best at. I love it when relationships are built off the foundation of learning from one another and growing together. It's super special!
