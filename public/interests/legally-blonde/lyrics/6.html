my thoughts

RAHHH this was undoubtedly the most frustrating to make because they seemingly changed it so much. It's hard to judge because I can't always tell if it was a mistake on one of the actors' parts or a change. If I'm being honest, I doubt this version count is super accurate... most of the changes are so small and they flip back and forth a ton. But I'm giving them the benefit of the doubt and marking them anyway.

As for the song itself, I really, really like it! I think all versions have their perks, but Love and War might be my favourite between the three of them. I just really like a beat!
